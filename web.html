Linguistics Research Apprentice Program
Adobe Illustrator

Along with transcribing the language of the Aleutian islands, I helped Edwin, our team's doctorate student mentor, draw up a background image for the online corpus we were working on. The goal was to keep it culturally correct, and after a several ideas of modern Aleutian art and simplified basket patterns, I decided to stick to the roots.

The very survival of the Aleuts rested on their hunting diet of seals, puffins, and whales among other foods. My team and I enjoyed encountering these animals during our readings of grammar books and old tales. I hoped to tie in the origins of Aleutian history and my team's enthusiasm for the Unangam Tunuu language in my web design.
