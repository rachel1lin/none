about me

My current interests consist of human-centered design, marketing, and linguistics. As of now, I am a research apprentice in the UC Berkeley Linguistics Department. Under the guidance of a PhD student, we are working on a project to create an online corpus for Unangam Tunuu (Aleut) in order to document and preserve the language of a historically oppressed indigenous people.

Some future pursuits include virtual reality, product design, and natural language processing.

Feel free to check out my resume for my past experiences.
